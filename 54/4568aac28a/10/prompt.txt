  What the Best Outbound Orgs Do Differently                                                                          
                                          
  From researching Kyle Coleman (5x5x5), Becc Holland (Flip the Script), Sam Nelson (Agoge), Josh Braun, and Jason    
  Lemkin — plus SDR onboarding playbooks from Vouris, Reply.io, and LeadIQ:                                           
                                  
  The universal pattern: Great onboarding follows a narrative, not a form. New SDRs learn in this order:              
  1. Customer problems first (not product features)             
  2. Who specifically (personas with depth)                                                                           
  3. What triggers action (timing/signals)                                                                            
  4. How we sound (voice + messaging)         
  5. Infrastructure last (it's plumbing, not strategy)                                                                
                                                                
  The current setup treats all 30+ fields as equal-weight flat config. The best outbound systems build knowledge in   
  layers where each layer informs the next.                                                                           
                                                                                                                      
  ---                                                                                                                 
  Proposed Redesign: 4 Conversations, Not 40 Questions                                                                
                                                                                                                      
  Instead of scattered config keys, the setup becomes 4 guided conversations — each one short, each one building on   
  the previous:                                                 
                                                                                                                      
  Conversation 1: "What problem do we solve?" (Identity + ICP)
                                                                                                                      
  One conversation captures everything about who we are and who we serve
                                                                                                                      
  Asks:                                                         
  - What does your company do? (→ company.description)                                                                
  - Who's it for? Company size, industry, stage, geography (→ ICP, all in one)
  - Who is it NOT for? (→ Anti-ICP, replaces separate disqualifiers)                                                  
  - What's your positioning line? (→ product.positioning)           
  - Any proof yet? (→ social_proof)                                                                                   
                                          
  Eliminates: separate company/product/ICP/qualify steps, geography duplication, disqualifier-ICP overlap             
                                                                                                                      
  Conversation 2: "Who do we talk to and when?" (Personas + Signals)                                                  
                                                                                                                      
  For each persona, build the full picture in one flow                                                                
                                                                                                                      
  For each title:                                                                                                     
  - What's their world like today? (→ pain points, current reality)                                                   
  - What changes when they use us? (→ transformation)              
  - What signals mean they're ready NOW? (→ timing signals)                                                           
                                                           
  Eliminates: separate persona, transformation, and signal seeding steps                                              
                                                                
  Conversation 3: "How do we sound?" (Voice + Structure)                                                              
                                          
  Quick — 2 minutes                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                      
  - Pick a tone: casual / professional / provocative (→ tone rules)                                                   
  - Email structure: what sections matter for first touch? (→ structure atom)                                         
  - What insight type leads? Tool stack, company moment, market? (→ insight_priority)
                                                                                                                      
  Eliminates: separate tone/voice/structure/insight_priority seeding
                                                                                                                      
  Conversation 4: "What are we aiming for?" (Goals + Infra)                                                           
                                                                                                                      
  Auto-detect infra, just confirm goals                                                                               
                                                                                                                      
  - Stage: validate PMF, grow pipeline, or scale? (→ kpi.goal + maturity)
  - Daily sends and weekly meeting targets (→ kpi targets)                                                            
  - Auto-detect: connected mailboxes, domain health, warmup status
  - Auto-detect: what tools are connected                                                                             
                                                                
  Eliminates: manual sending config, tools section, framework seeding flags                                           
                                                                                                                      
  What Gets Auto-Generated (not asked)                                                                                
                                                                                                                      
  After the 4 conversations, the system derives all knowledge atoms:
  - prospect_intelligence_framework (the mega-atom) — assembled from conversations 1-3                                
  - compliance rules — derived from geography                                         
  - structure/first_touch — from conversation 3                                                                       
  - All transformation/* atoms — from conversation 2            
  - signals/global — from conversation 2                                                                              
  - tone/global and positioning/global — from conversation 3 and 1
                                                                                                                      
  Forward-Looking Additions                                                                                           
                                                                                                                      
  Based on where outbound is heading (signal-based, multi-channel, AI-assisted), the setup should also capture:       
                                                                                                                      
  1. Anti-ICP explicitly — "Companies NOT to target" as a first-class concept (not just inverse of ICP). Becc Holland 
  and Sam Nelson both emphasize this saves more time than good targeting.                                             
  2. Signal plays — Not just "what signals exist" but "what do we DO when we see this signal?" Each signal gets a     
  mini-playbook.                                                                                                      
  3. Channel strategy — Email-only? Email + LinkedIn? Add phone for Tier 1? This shapes sequence design downstream.
  4. Competitive alternatives — What are prospects using today? (Apollo, Clay, Artisan, manual templates). This fuels 
  the "replaces" field in transformations and drives messaging angles.                                                
  5. Learning loops — The setup should declare what the system should learn from: reply sentiment, open rates, meeting
   conversion. This is the "gets smarter every session" promise built into the config.
